In the Absolute position property the yellow box slides down but the actual space of yellow box is occupied by the next element. In the Absolute property the box moves according to its parent position

In the position Relative property the box slides to down. But still the actual space of yellow box is keep empty and the next box cant override it. The space is still alloacated for the yellow box. In the Relative property the yellow box moves form its own position
